Taco Recipe

Description

Tacos are culinary marvel of Mexican origin. It is a meat nestled in warm tortillas

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Meat
  • Cooking oil
  • Onion
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Warm up tortillas on dry pan
  3. Place cooked meat on warmed tortillas
  4. Spread cut onion and cilantro over meat
