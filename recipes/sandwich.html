Sandwich

Description

The club sandwich is a classic and beloved triple-decker sandwich that basts a delighful combination of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 3 pieces of bread
  • mayonaise
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of turkey
  • 2 slices of ham
  • 1 slice of cheddar
  • Romaine lettuce

Steps

  1. Toast bread until golden and spread with mayonaise.
  2. Top 1 piece of bread with lettuce, tomato, and bacon.
  3. Place 1 bread on top of the bacon
  4. Top cheddar, ham, turkey, and the last piece of bread
